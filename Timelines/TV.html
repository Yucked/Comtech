Television Timeline

From mechanical disk to Smart TVs.

  • Paul Nipkow created a prototype that transferred images via rotating disk.

  • Boris Rosing and Campbell Swinton developed cathode ray & vacuum tube.

  • John Baird developed moving picture.

  • Birth of first television, W3XK, in United Sates.

  • Raytheon - Porthole shaped TV which included 12 channels.

  • United States introduction to color television and first TV remote is made.

  • RCA makes CT-100 available for consumers.

  • Broadcast of moon landing watched by 500 million people.

  • Introduction to giant screen projection.

  • Philco-Ford - Last vaccum-tube based TV set.

  • Japanese developed the first HDTV.

  • 98% of United States households owned a television set.

  • Sony developed Trinitron WEGA. Flat screen TV with built in VCR and DVD player.

  • Television now accepts signals in both analog and digital format.

  • Flat panel TV's take over the market.

  • Samsung introduces smart TV which supports internet connection and multiple built in applications.

  • Curved screen introduced by Samsung and LG.
